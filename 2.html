This is a comprehensive mock interview event designed to provide participants with a real interview experience, helping them sharpen their skills and boost their confidence for actual job interviews. This event aims to simulate the intensity and depth of a real job interview, covering various aspects such as scenario questions, behavioral questions, and practical exercises.

Flow of the Event:


Resume Review:

Participants should submit their resumes for review. The organizing team provides personalized feedback and tips for resume improvement. Pre-Interview Preparation Workshop: A brief workshop on interview best practices, do's and don'ts, and tips for success.

Mock Interview Rounds:

Participants will go through several rounds of mock interviews. Each round focuses on different aspects of the interview process, including:

Round 1: Behavioral Interview

Round 2: Scenario Questions

Round 3: Technical/Functional Skills

Round 4: Presentation or Task

Round 5: Q&A Session After each round, participants receive constructive feedback from interviewers. Evaluation criteria includes communication skills, problem-solving abilities, and suitability for the role.

  1. Participants are expected to maintain a high level of professionalism throughout the event.
  2. Participants must provide accurate and truthful information during the registration process
  3. Eligibility requirements must be met by all participants.
