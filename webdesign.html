WEBWONDERS-DESIGN CHALLEGE

Registration Fee :

Creating a website using HTML, CSS and other known frameworks in a dynamic blend of structure, Style, and interactivity. HTML lays the foundations; CSS enhances aesthetics and other frameworks adds functionality. Together, they enable designers and developers to craft captivating, user-friendly web experiences.

  • No. Of Participants : max 2 per team
  • Time duration : 10 am to 11:30 am
  • Held on : 23rd November 2023>

Design the Future: Web Mastery Unleashed" .Join us as we delve into the latest trends and innovations in web design, where creativity meets technology. Let your imagination soar as you explore the fusion of aesthetics and functionality. Redefine the boundaries of web design and embark on this creative journey

Flow of the Event:



Round 1:Email Submission

Participants will have to develop their frontend online and send their work via email. Selections will be made for the second round based on the submissions. Top 10 participants will be shortlisted for the final round.

Round 2: Webpage Development (Offline)

In this round, the selected participants are required to recreate the same web pages that they had submitted in the first round using frameworks like React, Node.js, or Angular.

    Round 1:


  1. You must use HTML and CSS to create a basic web page based on the given theme.
  2. The code will be obtained from GitHub.
  3. You are not allowed to use any frameworks.
  4. The platform is of your choice.

    Round 2:


  1. You should incorporate well-know frameworks into your HTML and CSS.
  2. Develop a full sample web page
  3. The platform is of your choice.
